About Us

Welcome to FoyleStarFishing, your source for all things related to oyster farming. We are dedicated to providing high-quality oysters and oyster seeds to meet your seafood needs.

European Oyster (Flat Oyster) 'Ostrea edulis'

The European oyster, also known as the flat oyster (Ostrea edulis), is a delicious and prized oyster variety. It is known for its unique flavor and is a delicacy in many European cuisines.

'Crassostrea gigas' - The Irish Rock Oyster

The 'Crassostrea gigas,' commonly known as the Irish rock oyster, is another excellent oyster variety. It's known for its robust flavor and is popular among oyster enthusiasts.

Oyster Seeds

We also offer oyster seeds for those looking to start their own oyster farms. Our high-quality oyster seeds are the perfect starting point for your oyster farming journey.
